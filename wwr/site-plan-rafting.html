Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Dry Oar Adventures website is to attract customers seeking whitewater rafting experiences on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers. The site will provide detailed information about available trips (difficulty, duration, pricing), highlight our company's experience and safety commitment, showcase the excitement of rafting through photos and testimonials, and facilitate easy booking or contact to drive revenue.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure-seeking tourists visiting the Glenwood Springs area, families looking for memorable outdoor activities (ages 8+ for milder trips), corporate groups seeking team-building exercises, and local residents. They range in age from 18-60, typically have moderate to high disposable income, and are interested in adventure, nature, and outdoor recreation. They need clear information on trip options, safety measures, what to bring, and how to book. They will access the site via desktop/laptop for planning and mobile devices for quick information checks.
